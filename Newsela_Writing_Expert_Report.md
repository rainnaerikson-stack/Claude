## Executive Summary

Newsela Writing is an AI-powered writing practice and feedback tool for grades 3–12, embedded within the broader Newsela platform. It uses **Luna AI** (Newsela's proprietary AI assistant) to deliver real-time, rubric-aligned feedback on student writing without rewriting for them. It won the **2025 CODiE Award** for Best Writing Instructional Solution and is backed by **ESSA Tier III research** showing an average 18-point ELA score boost when used regularly.

## Assignment Creation Flow (6 Steps)

**Step 1 — Length**
- **Short Answer:** Single paragraph, evidence from a text required
- **Long-form:** Multi-paragraph with intro, body, conclusion

**Step 2 — Type of Writing (8 genres)**
1. Literary Analysis — perspective/interpretation using textual evidence
2. Argumentative/Persuasive — position + textual evidence
3. Personal Narrative — story from personal experience
4. Expository — explains a topic
5. Informative/Informational — informs readers about a topic
6. Opinion — expresses and explains a student opinion
7. Main/Central Idea — conveys the main idea of a text
8. Inference/Conclusion — identifies and explains an idea from a text

**Step 3 — Newsela Content Source (Optional)**
- Teacher can link a Newsela article URL so students see the article and writing side-by-side

**Step 4 — Assignment Prompt**
- Teacher writes their own prompt OR enters a topic/book to have **Luna AI generate prompt suggestions**
- AI prompt disclaimer: *"Recommendations are generated using A.I. It may produce inaccurate information about people, places, or facts."*
- Guidance tips: include a clear question, mention key text(s), limit format instructions

**Steps 5 & 6** — (Class assignment and settings — requires school subscription to fully access)

## Student Writing Editor

### Key Interface Elements
- **Prompt display** at top with collapse/expand toggle
- **Rich text editor toolbar:** Bold, Italic, Underline, Font Family/Size, Text Color, Align, Indent, Insert Link, Insert Horizontal Line
- **Color-coded sentence annotations** in the writing body:
  - 🟡 `background` — contextual framing sentence
  - 🔵 `add. prompt` — addresses the prompt
  - 🟢 `evidence` — textual evidence cited
  - 🟣 `reasoning` — logical reasoning/analysis
  - ⚪ `summary` — closing/summary sentence
- **Teacher comment panel** (left side, visible to student)
- **Version History tab** (NEW feature)

### Right Panel — Sentence Checklist
- Lists each sentence type the assignment requires (e.g., Reasoning, Add. Prompt, Background, Evidence, Summary)
- **"Check for sentences ✦"** button triggers Luna AI to classify each sentence and mark completion
- Completion indicator: "5 of 5 complete" with checkmarks
- Luna mascot (astronaut robot) guides student to "rubric meters" after checklist completion

## ML Feedback System — Key Findings

*(Full analysis in `ML_Feedback_Analysis.md`)*

**Strengths:**
- Sentence classification (label-essay) is pedagogically transparent and unique vs. competitors
- Rubric-anchored feedback — each of 6 criteria gets distinct glows/grows
- Mastery-adaptive: top-scoring essays receive extension-oriented feedback; mid-scoring get corrective
- Verb tense inconsistency detection is impressive

**Critical Issues Found (Live Testing):**
1. **Organization grow bug** — short-answer essays receive advice to "add a separate introduction, body, and conclusion," which is structurally impossible for single-paragraph format
2. **No logical reasoning evaluation** — Luna AI detects evidence *presence* but not whether reasoning *supports* the thesis; missed a reasoning sentence that contradicted the student's argument
3. **Cross-category glow duplication** — identical phrase ("Your introduction effectively sets the stage") appeared verbatim in both Organization and Conventions feedback panels
4. **Formulaic opener** — every grow begins "To make this even stronger..." across all 6 criteria

**Feature Flags Observed:**
`3G_SHORT_FUNCTIONALITY`, `3G_LONG_FUNCTIONALITY`, `EVIDENCE_DETECTION`, `SENTECE_LABEL_SELECTOR_SHORT/LONG_FORM` *(typo in flag names)*, `STREAMING_FEATURE`, `DBQ_FEATURE` *(unreleased writing type)*, `LABEL_DESCRIPTORS_FEATURE`, `STANDARDS_FILTER`

## Competitive Baseline Summary

Newsela Writing occupies the **AI-assisted writing feedback** space for K-12, positioned between:
- **General AI tools** (ChatGPT, etc.) — Newsela differentiates by NOT rewriting, being teacher-controlled, and curriculum-aligned
- **Traditional rubric grading** — Newsela automates the feedback loop while keeping the teacher in control
- **Other EdTech writing tools** (Writable, Quill, NoRedInk, Turnitin Feedback Studio) — direct competitors to benchmark against in subsequent reports
